Our Teams

Competitive teams representing Miracle Basketball in national leagues.

Player Development Pathway

Academy → Development Squad → Competitive Team → National League

Miracle Eagles

The Miracle Eagles compete in Uganda's Division One League and represent the men's competitive program within Miracle Basketball.

The team continues to strengthen tactical discipline, physical preparation, and teamwork as they pursue promotion to the National Basketball League (NBL).

Miracle Eaglets

The Miracle Eaglets compete in Uganda’s National Basketball League, representing elite-level women’s basketball.

The team inspires the next generation of female athletes within the academy while competing at the highest level of Ugandan basketball.
