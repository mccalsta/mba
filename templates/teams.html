Our Teams

Competitive teams representing Miracle Basketball in national leagues.

Miracle Basketball Club

Our competitive structure reflects a commitment to sustainable growth and structured progression for young athletes.

Through disciplined training and mentorship, players move through a clear development pathway that prepares them for elite competition.

Academy → Development Squad → Competitive Team → National League

Miracle Eagles

The Miracle Eagles compete in Uganda's Division One League and represent the men's competitive program within Miracle Basketball.

The team is steadily strengthening tactical cohesion, physical preparation, and team unity as they pursue promotion to the National Basketball League (NBL).

The Eagles embody ambition, discipline, and commitment to both performance excellence and strong character.

Miracle Eaglets

The Miracle Eaglets compete in Uganda’s National Basketball League, representing elite-level women’s basketball.

The team demonstrates composure, leadership, and competitive excellence while inspiring the next generation of female athletes within the Miracle Basketball academy.

The Eaglets serve as ambassadors for both high-level sport and faith-centred leadership within the basketball community.
