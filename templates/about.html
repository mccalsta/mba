About Miracle Basketball

Developing athletes, shaping character, and inspiring purpose.

Our Foundation

Miracle Basketball was established over two decades ago as a ministry initiative under Robert Kayanja Ministries, founded and led by Pastor Robert Kayanja.

The vision has always been to use basketball as a structured platform for mentorship, discipline, and youth development.

For more than 25 years the organization has provided young athletes with a safe and disciplined environment where talent is refined and purpose is discovered.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To develop skilled athletes and principled leaders through basketball, mentorship, and Christ-centred values.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of disciplined, confident and purpose-driven young people who influence society positively through sport.

Our Core Values

Faith

We honour God in our conduct and competition.

Excellence

We pursue high standards in preparation and performance.

Unity

We value teamwork, accountability and cooperation.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, respect and responsibility.

Service

We invest in community upliftment and youth empowerment.
