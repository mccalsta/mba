About Miracle Basketball Academy

Developing athletes, shaping character, and inspiring purpose.

Our Foundation

Miracle Basketball was established over two decades ago as a ministry initiative under Robert Kayanja Ministries, founded and led by Pastor Robert Kayanja, who serves as the program’s Founder and Patron.

From the beginning, the vision has been to use sport as a structured platform for spiritual, emotional, and personal transformation. Rooted in Christian values, we guide young people toward excellence not only in performance, but in character, identity, and faith-centred living.

For more than 25 years, Miracle Basketball has provided boys and girls with a safe and disciplined environment where talent is refined and purpose is discovered.

Our Mission

To develop skilled athletes and principled leaders through basketball, mentorship, and Christ-centred values.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of disciplined, confident, and purpose-driven young people who influence society positively through sport and character.

Our Core Values
