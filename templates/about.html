About Miracle Basketball

Developing athletes, shaping character, and inspiring purpose.

Our Foundation

Miracle Basketball was established over two decades ago as a ministry initiative under Robert Kayanja Ministries, founded and led by Pastor Robert Kayanja, who serves as the program’s Founder and Patron.

From the beginning, the vision has been to use sport as a structured platform for spiritual, emotional, and personal transformation. Rooted in Christian values, we guide young people toward excellence not only in performance, but in character, identity, and faith-centred living.

For more than 25 years, Miracle Basketball has provided boys and girls with a safe and disciplined environment where talent is refined and purpose is discovered. Through the integration of athletic development, mentorship, education support, and community outreach, every participant is intentionally guided and empowered.

Today, Miracle Basketball stands as a multi-generational platform of influence, with alumni competing nationally and serving in leadership and coaching roles across Uganda’s basketball community — a testament to both competitive excellence and lives shaped with integrity.

Our Mission

To develop skilled athletes and principled leaders through basketball, mentorship, and Christ-centred values.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of disciplined, confident, and purpose-driven young people who influence society positively through sport and character.

Our Core Values

Faith

We honour God in our conduct, training, and competition.

Excellence

We pursue high standards in preparation and performance.

Unity

We value cooperation, accountability, and collective strength.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, respect, and responsibility.

Service

We invest in community upliftment and youth empowerment.
