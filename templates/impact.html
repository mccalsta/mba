A Legacy of Transformation

For more than twenty-five years, Miracle Basketball has served as a youth development ministry under Robert Kayanja Ministries.

What began as a vision to use basketball as a tool for mentorship has grown into a multi-generational platform of influence.

Many athletes who began their journey in the academy now compete nationally and serve as coaches and mentors within Uganda’s basketball community.
