Our Impact

A 25-year legacy of transforming young lives through basketball and mentorship.

A Legacy of Transformation

For over twenty-five years, Miracle Basketball has served as a ministry platform under Robert Kayanja Ministries.

What began as a vision to use sport as a tool for youth guidance has grown into a multi-generational movement of transformation. Many athletes who started their journey within Miracle now compete nationally, coach across leagues, and continue to influence the game with integrity.

More Than Trophies

Our greatest impact is not measured by championships alone, but by the lives that have been shaped through discipline, mentorship, and faith-centred leadership.

Within Miracle Basketball, the court becomes a classroom where athletes learn unity, responsibility, perseverance, confidence, and purpose.

Leadership & Mentorship

Faithful leadership has sustained the vision of Miracle Basketball.

  • Founder & Patron: Pastor Robert Kayanja
  • Head Coach: Coach Kizza Joel Atkins
  • Mentor: Mr. Katabi Arnold (Former Miracle Player)
  • Mentor: Mr. Tony Drileba – 2025 UBUNTU Award Recipient

Supported by committed staff and mentors, the program continues to raise athletes who compete with excellence and live with strong character.

Looking Forward

With a strong spiritual foundation and structured development pathway, Miracle Basketball continues to nurture a new generation of athletes and leaders.

Our mission remains clear: to develop skilled players, principled leaders, and young people grounded in faith for generations to come.
