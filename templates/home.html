A 25-Year Legacy of Transformation

For over two decades Miracle Basketball has served as a ministry platform under Robert Kayanja Ministries, shaping disciplined athletes and principled young leaders across Uganda.

Many athletes who began their journey within Miracle now compete nationally, coach across leagues, and continue to influence the game with integrity and leadership.
